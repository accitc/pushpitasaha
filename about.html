THE NORTHERN LIGHTS- What is it?


The Aurora Borealis (named after the Roman goddess of dawn, Aurora, and the Greek name for the north wind, Boreas) Is there anything more remarkable on this planet of ours than Mother Nature? Here, at the Northern Lights we’re inclined to think not. From towering mountain peaks to thundering waterfalls, underwater wonders to geological marvels, we could spend a thousand lifetimes exploring this amazing world. However, of all the incredible places to visit and stunning sites to see on planet Earth, there is one natural phenomenon that always comes out top in the “bucket list” charts: the Aurora Borealis or, as they are more commonly known, the Northern Lights.



OUR MISSION:


This website came to be because we love northern lights but we live in Bangladesh where auroras don’t appear .We would like to present to you the beauty and facts about the gorgeously luminous northern sky The Aurora Borealis is surely Mother Nature’s greatest spectacle.
Our mission is to take you to the depth of the science behind the Northern lights. Wherever you are best, of luck in your aurora chasing.!
